BLOCKCHAIN

Blockchain is a decentralized and distributed digital ledger technology that securely records transactions across a network of computers. It ensures transparency, immutability, and security by using cryptographic principles to create a chain of blocks, where each block contains a record of multiple transactions .

Crypto
Currency

Cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency that uses cryptography for security, making it nearly impossible to counterfeit or double-spend. It operates on decentralized networks based on blockchain technology, enabling secure, transparent, and peer-to-peer transactions without the need for intermediaries like banks
